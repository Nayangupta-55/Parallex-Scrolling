The Concept

Depth Without
Limits

Parallax scrolling creates the illusion of depth by moving foreground and background layers at different rates. This project transforms a static webpage into an immersive narrative where scrolling becomes exploration.

Every layer, every element, every transition is intentional designed to guide your eye and hold your attention.

What's Inside

Key Features

◈

Multi-Layer Parallax

Background, midground, and foreground elements move at distinct speeds, creating true three-dimensional depth on a flat screen.

⟳

Scroll-Triggered Animations

Elements animate into view precisely as you reach them powered by GSAP ScrollTrigger for frame-perfect timing.

◉

Particle System

A dynamic canvas-based star field responds to your scroll velocity, adding a living, breathing atmosphere to the experience.

⬡

Responsive Design

Fluid layouts and adaptive parallax intensities ensure the experience looks and feels great on every device size.

▲

Smooth Transitions

Section transitions use GSAP timelines with eased motion curves , no janky jumps, only fluid cinematic movement.

✦

Performance Optimised

requestAnimationFrame loops, will-change hints, and debounced resize listeners keep every animation silky at 60 fps.
